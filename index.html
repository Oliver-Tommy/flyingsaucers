About Us

Flying Saucers is a unique pottery café where you can unleash your creativity while enjoying a cup of coffee. Whether you're a seasoned artist or a beginner, we have something for everyone!

Bristol's largest and only dedicated Painting Pottery Café. We are located off the Clifton Triangle.

Our Services

Items professionally glazed and fired. Collect within a week, and keep a lifetime!

How it works

  • There is a £6 studio fee per adult and £5 per child.
  • You then choose your item, a plate, bowl, mug, or even piggy bank, unicorn, fairy or dinosaur from our extensive range, these are priced individually.
  • Your session will last 2 hours.
  • Decide how you'll paint it. We have books for inspiration if you're stuck.
  • Sit & paint using our brushes or sponge stamps - our staff will help with advice.
  • Have a drink & a snack whilst you create.
